About Us

Entering our tent, you will be enchanted by the baroque decor, intimate and shimmering red velvet, beveled mirrors, brass, crystal chandeliers, gilding, balconies worthy of an opera.



Laughter, emotion, thrills, magic, great numbers presented by artists whose reputation is second to none. The 7th generation of the Bormann Moreno family is eager to welcome you to this dream setting where you will find the greatest circus disciplines that have evolved over time while respecting the family tradition. If you come to see us on your birthday, tell us at the checkout. We will reserve a surprise during the show ...


In your garden, your company, your nightclub ... Cirque Bormann moves and comes to enchant your private party for your prestige and the greatest happiness of your guests !



It is possible to rent the marquee after a show to continue the party with your friends (birthday, pot, evening suprise). For a complete privatization, contact us.
